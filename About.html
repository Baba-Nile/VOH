Who We Are

Creating lasting impact

BintiCare Foundation is a Non-Profit organization founded in 2012. We aim to create a future where all women and the girls have the tools and power to reach their full potential.

Core Values

  • Accountability and transparency in all our actions
  • Openness to feedback, ideas, and continuous improvement
  • Service to the poor and underserved communities
  • Stakeholder participation for community-based solutions
Our Foundation

The Binticare Foundation DNA

Our work is built on three interconnected pillars that shape transformation, restore dignity, and create sustainable change in the lives of girls and young women.

Dignity

We believe that every girl should know her worth and that of others. Respectful development means we do not see the girls as numbers or statistics, but as valuable individual humans. Enhancing their dignity means, we challenge through classrooms and communities the words, situations and imagery that lead to low self-value, discrimination, violence and injustice.

Purpose

We believe that every girl should be able to lead a life with an informed sense of direction. Transforming the lives of individuals and communities means, we go all out to assist valunarable young women and girls to find trully meaningful, fulfilling and fruitful pursuits for themselves and their community, and provide practical guides on how they can fulfil their goals.

Belonging

We believe that a girl can become what she want to be and long for more without feeling alone, insecure, unsafe or helpless.

Our Initiatives

Transforming Lives Through Action

We implement community-driven initiatives that restore dignity, strengthen purpose, and create lasting impact for girls and women.

Outreach Programs

These initiative reach out to sensitize girls and women to understand their dignity, purpose and belonging and to live a safe, fulfilled, and productive life through:-

  • Dignity Sesssions
  • Medical Camps
  • Maternity Support Program
  • Prison Support Program

Rescue Missions

In order to be able to rescue vulnerable girls, provide a safe and psychosocial support and facilitate their access to education, we can carry out the following activities that promote this objective:-

  • Binti House(The Safety Net)
  • Schooling Program
  • Household Visitation Program(The Rescue)

Skills & Capacity Building

The objective is to intergrate the educational and livelihood skills of girls and women through training for socio-economic self-reliance to transform women's lives. This initiative involves:-

  • Satelite Training Hubs
  • Village Teens Mothers Program
