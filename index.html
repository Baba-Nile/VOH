Who We Are

Creating Dignity,
Purpose & Belonging

BintiCare Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering girls and young women. We believe every girl deserves dignity, education, and the chance to reach her full potential.

Our Purpose

To restore dignity and confidence among girls through sustainable community support programs.

Our Mission

To empower young women & girls with tools for social and economic transformation in their lives and communities.

Our Vision

A world where girls have equal opportunity, dignity, and the power to fully realize their potential.
